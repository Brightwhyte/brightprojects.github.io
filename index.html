April 22, 2017

Unilever
Canteen Project

In this project, I worked on building a new canteen ordering system of the UNILEVER Company. The painpoint of the company was that it has over 1500 employees which are spread across 12 floors and there are only 2 canteens to cater for all of them. Each canteen seat around 150 employees at a time. Employees have to rush to get their meals at break which is just a hour and most of them end up not getting a table to eat, the canteens find it hard to sastify everyone with their menu of the day and most of the time, they end up throwing away food that was not purchased. So in this project, I helped in mapping the AS_IS and To_BE process. And my Team and I helped in deloying an app that would enable employees to order their food and either get it delivered to them in their various offices or get a table at any of the canteen.
